Our Founders

The visionaries behind Mirabelle Hotel & Suites

Mr. Agu Ozoemena & Mrs. Agu Gloria

Mirabelle Hotel & Suites was founded by the visionary couple, Mr. Agu Ozoemena and his wife Mrs. Agu Gloria. Their dream was to create a sanctuary of hospitality that would stand the test of time, providing exceptional care and comfort to every guest.

"We believe that true hospitality is not just about providing a place to stay, but about creating a home away from home where every guest feels valued, cared for, and at peace."

For years, the Agu family has championed the development of hospitality in Lagos, dedicating their lives to creating spaces where people can find tranquility, generosity, and the warmth of genuine care. Their commitment to excellence has made Mirabelle Hotel a landmark of luxury and comfort in the heart of Okokomaiko.

Today, their legacy continues through their children and the dedicated team at Mirabelle, who uphold the founding principles of exceptional service, attention to detail, and heartfelt hospitality that has become the hallmark of our establishment.

Our History

A legacy of hospitality spanning generations

From Humble Beginnings to Excellence

Mirabelle Hotel & Suites began as a modest hotel in the 2009, founded by Mr. Agu Ozoemena and his wife Mrs. Agu Gloria. What started as a small family business with just five rooms has grown into one of Lagos' most respected hospitality establishments.

Through years of dedication and an unwavering commitment to service excellence, the Agu family expanded their vision, transforming the original guesthouse into the luxurious hotel we know today. Each expansion was guided by their core belief that every guest deserves to experience the warmth of Nigerian hospitality at its finest.

Located at Trinity Bus Stop in Okokomaiko, our hotel has become a landmark destination for both business and leisure travelers. We've hosted countless guests from around the world, each leaving with stories of our exceptional service and the tranquil atmosphere that defines the Mirabelle experience.

Today, while we've grown in size and amenities, we remain true to our founders' vision of providing a home away from home where care, tranquility, and generosity are at the heart of everything we do.
